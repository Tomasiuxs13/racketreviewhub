Technologies

The high balance and diamond shape excel at the net, where aggressive players can dominate. Quick exchanges favor this racket, and the power allows you to finish points decisively.

On Smash
This is where the racket truly excels. With impressive energy transfer, it enables powerful smashes and overheads, helping to finish off points with authority. The combination of the diamond shape, high balance, and carbon fiber construction creates impressive power on smashes.
